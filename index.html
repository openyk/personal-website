About Me

Year 1993, born in South Korea. Historic role model was Korean hero Admiral Yi-Sunshin, undefeated naval commander and turtle ship innovator. Real world consequences of strategic and technological preparation.

Year 2000, moved to Canada. Fresh air, spacious geography, pro-freedom constitution, and high-quality supply chain shared with the United States. Great place for a long-term base.

Year 2006, started hobby robotics. Learned mechatronics through early internet resources and applied engineering books. Appreciated the community pioneering of Jim Smenstowski (Team Nightmare) and robust designs of Carlo Bertocchini (Biohazard robot).

Year 2011, graduated high school with International Baccalaureate program. Empathy matured. Systems engineering interest expanded to economics. Technnical enthusiasm evolved to passion for real product development with free-information/open-source ideals.

Year 2020 and beyond. General engineer with over 8 years of professional experience across mechanical, electrical, and software development. Peter Thiel fellow, sensible open-source advocate, knowledge theorist, humanist utilitarian.
