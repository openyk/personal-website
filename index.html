About this Website

Personal websites are limited for long-term knowledge-sharing because they are temporary (dies with the person), niche value (fragments of one person's ideas), and hard to naturally find (unless the content is high SEO or the person is specifically searched for).

That said, they create opportunities for interested people to passively explore and build rapport with like-minded individuals (beyond the professional curtain of typical public-profile platforms). Personal websites can build natural unity at the individual level.

Productivity

Year, 365 days. Day, 24 hours, Healthy sleep, 8 hours average per day.

Every day, 0.27% of the year. Every day, 16 hours of healthy uptime.

Every minute, doubly useful for concurrent physical action and mental exertion.

Every action, gated by possibility awareness (know available actions and consequences), rational confidence (determine one action as good enough), mental discipline (actually do the best known course of action). Example, cooking alarm. It rings, you consider the state of the kitchen (awareness). You know you need to turn off the stove to prevent the food from burning (confidence). Tired or not, you walk there and turn the stove off (discipline).
